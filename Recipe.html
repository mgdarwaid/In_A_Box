Blueberry Smoothie

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup frozen blueberries
  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup almond milk
  • 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Ice cubes (optional)
  • Fresh blueberries and mint leaves for garnish

Nutrition Information:

Serving Size: 1 Blueberry Smoothie

  • Calories: 200
  • Total Fat: 3g
  • Saturated Fat: 1g
  • Cholesterol: 5mg
  • Sodium: 80mg
  • Total Carbohydrate: 40g
  • Dietary Fiber: 6g
  • Sugars: 26g
  • Protein: 8g

Cooking Guidelines:

  1. In a blender, combine the frozen blueberries, ripe banana, Greek yogurt, almond milk, honey or maple syrup, and vanilla extract.
  2. Blend on high speed until smooth and creamy. If desired, add a few ice cubes to make the smoothie colder and thicker.
  3. Pour the smoothie into a glass and garnish with fresh blueberries and mint leaves.
  4. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Chocolate Coffee

price-20$

coffee

Ingredients:

  • 2 Mahi Mahi fillets
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 8 small tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1/2 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup diced red onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • Lime wedges for serving
  • Salsa or hot sauce (optional)

Nutrition Information:

Serving Size: 1 Mahi Taco

  • Calories: 250
  • Total Fat: 8g
  • Saturated Fat: 1g
  • Cholesterol: 60mg
  • Sodium: 350mg
  • Total Carbohydrate: 23g
  • Dietary Fiber: 3g
  • Sugars: 2g
  • Protein: 22g

Cooking Guidelines:

  1. Preheat your grill or stovetop grill pan to medium-high heat.
  2. In a small bowl, mix together the olive oil, chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Brush the Mahi Mahi fillets with the spice mixture, coating both sides.
  4. Place the fillets on the grill and cook for about 4-5 minutes per side or until the fish is opaque and flakes easily with a fork.
  5. Remove the Mahi Mahi from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes. Then, flake the fish into small pieces using a fork.
  6. Warm the tortillas on the grill for a few seconds on each side.
  7. Assemble the tacos by placing a spoonful of the flaked Mahi Mahi onto each tortilla.
  8. Top with shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, red onion, and chopped cilantro.
  9. Squeeze some fresh lime juice over the tacos and serve with salsa or hot sauce if desired.

Chinese Dumplings

Ingredients:

  • 1 package dumpling wrappers
  • 1/2 pound ground pork
  • 1 cup finely chopped cabbage
  • 2 green onions, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup chopped shiitake mushrooms
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • Water for sealing the dumplings
  • Vegetable oil for frying or steaming

Nutrition Information:

Serving Size: 4 Dumplings

  • Calories: 150
  • Total Fat: 7g
  • Saturated Fat: 2g
  • Cholesterol: 25mg
  • Sodium: 350mg
  • Total Carbohydrate: 15g
  • Dietary Fiber: 1g
  • Sugars: 1g
  • Protein: 8g

Cooking Guidelines:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the ground pork, chopped cabbage, green onions, shiitake mushrooms, minced garlic, soy sauce, sesame oil, grated ginger, salt, and black pepper. Mix well until all ingredients are evenly incorporated.
  2. Take a dumpling wrapper and place a tablespoon of the pork filling in the center.
  3. Dip your finger in water and moisten the edges of the wrapper.
  4. Fold the wrapper in half, sealing the edges tightly and creating a half-moon shape. Pleat the edges if desired for a decorative look.
  5. Repeat the process with the remaining wrappers and filling.
  6. To fry the dumplings, heat a tablespoon of vegetable oil in a skillet over medium heat. Place the dumplings in the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes until the bottoms are golden brown. Add water to the skillet, cover, and steam the dumplings for an additional 5 minutes until cooked through.
  7. To steam the dumplings, place them in a steamer basket lined with parchment paper. Steam over high heat for 10-12 minutes until cooked through.
  8. Serve the Chinese dumplings hot with dipping sauce of your choice, such as soy sauce, vinegar, or chili oil.
